Welcome to Cargo!

Cargo is an S3 Gateway and the new Hack Club CDN. We are moving away from traditional CDNs to give you more power and flexibility.

1GB Free Storage

Every Hack Clubber on Slack gets 1 GB for free.

Need more? Ship projects! Storage can be used for public CDN hosting or private S3 buckets for your projects.

When you turn 18, we'll archive your data and send you a download link before deleting it. But that's a problem for future you!

It's Just S3

You can use almost all standard S3 functions. We store everyone's data in one massive bucket, but to you, it feels like your own private bucket.

You're using a gateway, but you'll never notice the difference. Just create buckets and ship code!

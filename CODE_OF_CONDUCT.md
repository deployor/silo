# Be good to each other

Silo is for people making things. We want this repository to feel like a place where someone can ask a slightly awkward question, share a half-formed idea, or submit their first pull request without getting dunked on.

Be kind. Assume good intent. Give useful feedback. Do not harass, discriminate against, threaten, dox, or deliberately embarrass people.

If something here makes that harder, email [security@deployor.dev](mailto:security@deployor.dev). We will listen and handle it quietly.

That is the whole rulebook. Please help us keep it true.
